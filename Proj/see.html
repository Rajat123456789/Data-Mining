Company Overview
Arcadia is a pioneering climate technology company that is driving the transition to a zero-carbon economy by providing unprecedented access to global energy data from over 9,500 utility providers across 52 countries. Their platform not only enables companies to assess and act on their environmental impact but also fosters the development of advanced energy products and climate tech solutions. With a diverse team of technologists, data scientists, and energy experts, Arcadia is also addressing energy injustice and promoting economic growth through the management of the nation's leading community solar program.
